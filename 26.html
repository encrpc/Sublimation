What is Sublimation Printing?

Sublimation printing uses heat and pressure to convert dye into gas, infusing ink directly into fabric fibers. The result is full-color, breathable graphics that won't crack, peel, or fade — ideal for team uniforms, custom lifestyle apparel, and performance wear.

  • Long-lasting color
  • Unlimited design possibilities
  • Breathable & comfortable finish
  • Eco-friendly process (low waste)
